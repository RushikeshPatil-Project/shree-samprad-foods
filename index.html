Storage & Cooking Instructions

Get the best out of your frozen green peas

Storage Instructions

  • Always store in the freezer compartment of your refrigerator
  • Keep frozen at -18°C or below for optimal quality
  • Once opened, reseal the pack tightly or transfer to an airtight container
  • Best before 12 months from packaging when stored at -18°C
  • Do not refreeze once thawed

Cooking Instructions

  • Cut open the pack & loosen peas gently
  • Take out required quantity and immediately return balance to freezer
  • For immediate cooking, thaw peas in fresh water for 10-15 minutes
  • Avoid using microwave for defrosting
  • Cook as per your recipe - no need to overcook as peas are already tender
  • Perfect for curries, pulao, soups, salads, and snacks
